Arts Therapy Integration

I incorporate various art modalities into therapeutic practice to help clients express emotions that may be difficult to articulate verbally. These creative processes facilitate self-discovery, emotional release, and personal transformation.

Music Therapy Approaches

I integrate music and rhythm into therapeutic sessions to help clients regulate emotions, reduce anxiety, and enhance self-expression. These techniques are particularly effective for clients who respond well to auditory and kinesthetic modalities.

Violin Therapy
